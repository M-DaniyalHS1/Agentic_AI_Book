# Introduction to Physical AI

Welcome to the **AI-Native Digital Textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook bridges the gap between software intelligence and physical embodiment. You'll learn:

- How to build robots that think and act intelligently
- ROS 2 architecture for robotic control
- Simulation environments (Gazebo, Unity, NVIDIA Isaac Sim)
- Vision-Language-Action pipelines
- How to create reproducible robotics experiments

## Why Physical AI?

Modern AI education often treats software and robotics as separate disciplines. This textbook unifies them, teaching you how perception, reasoning, action, and human interaction work together in Physical AI systems.
